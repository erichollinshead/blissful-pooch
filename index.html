About

My name is Jennifer Astacio, owner and baker at Blissful Pooch Barkery. Why did I start making these delicious dog treats?

A few years ago I changed my lifestyle to a gluten free and all organic diet. I can see and feel the huge difference it has made in my health and in my life. I now realize how important it is to put the best ingredients in your body for a better you! I want to do the same for our fur babies! They deserve the greatest life has to offer and that starts with food! I created delicious and nutritious homemade dog treats that your pooch will love. All treats are full of flavor and homemade right from my kitchen. Never any preservatives and most of all MADE WITH LOVE!

Thanks for visiting my website. -Jen

Banana Oatmeal Cookie Treats

Ingredients: Oat flour, coconut flour, pumpkin puree, peanut butter, cinnamon, coconut oil, water, eggs and honey. Oat flour is a great alternative carbohydrate for dogs who may be sensitive to wheat. Oats contain vitamin B, which helps maintain a healthy coat!

Blueberry Ginger Bliss Treats

Ingredients: Oat flour, quinoa flour, blueberries, chia seeds, cinnamon, ginger, coconut oil, water, eggs and honey. Quinoa flour is a good source of a range of minerals including copper, folate, iron, magnesium, phosphorous and zinc. It also contains all 9 essential amino acids which are important for the health of your dog!

Coconut Delight Treats

Ingredients: Coconut flour, coconut, coconut milk, coconut oil, chia seeds, eggs and honey. Chia seeds contribute to a healthy coat and are also a good source of minerals such as magnesium, copper and zinc! Coconut oil improves your dogs skin and coat, it also improves digestion!

Peanut Butter Pumpkin Spice Treats

Ingredients: Oat flour, coconut flour, pumpkin puree, peanut butter, cinnamon, coconut oil, water, eggs and honey. Oat flour is a great alternative carbohydrate for dogs who may be sensitive to wheat. Oats contain vitamin B, which helps maintain a healthy coat!

Peanut Butter Pooch Treats

Ingredients: Oat flour, sorghum flour, flax seeds, peanut butter, water, eggs and honey. Sorghum flour provides many health benefits for your pooch. Not only is it gluten free and high in antioxidants, it is also rich in B1, niacin, iron, zinc and dietary fiber B2!

Sweet Potato Apple Pie Treats

Ingredients: Oat flour, coconut flour, sweet potato, apple, flax seeds, cinnamon, coconut oil, water, eggs and maple syrup. Flaxseeds assist in your pooch's digestive health. It is naturally rich in fiber. It is especially important for maintaining a healthy weight. It is also an excellent antioxidant for your fur baby!
