About

My name is Jennifer Astacio, owner and baker at Blissful Pooch Barkery. Why did I start making these delicious dog treats?

A few years ago I changed my lifestyle to a gluten free and all organic diet. I can see and feel the huge difference it has made in my health and in my life. I now realize how important it is to put the best ingredients in your body for a better you! I want to do the same for our fur babies! They deserve the greatest life has to offer and that starts with food! I created delicious and nutritious homemade dog treats that your pooch will love. All treats are full of flavor and homemade right from my kitchen. No preservatives.

Thanks for visiting my website. -Jen

Ingredients: Oat flour, quinoa flour, blueberries, chia seeds, cinnamon, ginger, coconut oil, water, eggs and honey. Quinoa flour is a good source of a range of minerals including copper, folate, iron, magnesium, phosphorous and zinc.
